Welcome to my website, I'm Mengna Wu. I was born in China. I'm currently a hands-on Graphic designer and Front-end developer.

My design philosophy is projecting functionality into aesthetically pleasing. I believe the two of them walk side by side on the trip of human cognizing the world. Introducing different cultures to each other is one of my design methodologies. Difference means novelty, novelty means change, change means evolution. The only way to keep your design modern, is to keep trying new things.
