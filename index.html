Hi, I'm Mengna Wu. I was born in China. I'm currently a hands-on UI designer. Check out some of my work below.

I'm training myself to a web developer. Because I love to execute all those fabulous visual effects myself and keep them straight to the design goals I wanted to achieve as a UI designer.

My design philosophy is project functionality into aesthetically pleasing. I believe the two of them are two sides of one coin. Introducing different cultures to each other is one of my design methodologies. Difference means novelty, novelty means change, change means evolution.

The only way to keep your design modern, is to keep trying new things.
