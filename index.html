Welcome, I'm Mengna Wu. I was born in China and I currently live in Florida. I'm a hands-on Graphic designer and Front-end developer and just graduated with a Masters in Digital Marketing Communication at FSU. Thanks for visiting my website, which I created to represent my skills in design and web development.

When it comes to design, functionality combined with aestheticism is important. I believe the two of them walk side by side on the journey of human interaction with the world. Introducing different cultures to each other is one of my design methodologies. Difference means novelty, novelty means change, change means evolution. The only way to keep your design modern, is to keep trying new things.
